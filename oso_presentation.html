Digital asset protection · Detail

Three threats OSO defends against

OSO's security layer is built around three core attack surfaces — all protected from day one, with more layers coming.

Threat 01 · Content theft
Data & script protection
Book content is served as encrypted streams — not raw files. Copy-paste is disabled in the reader. Scripts and formatting code are obfuscated so content cannot be scraped or cloned.
Protected now
Threat 02 · Fake engagement
Anti-bot for view counts
Automated bots can fake reads, inflate rankings, and corrupt revenue. OSO fingerprints every session — headless browsers, rapid-fire reads, and suspicious patterns are flagged and blocked in real time.
Protected now
Threat 03 · Account takeover
Access credential security
All passwords are hashed. Sessions use short-lived encrypted tokens. Partners and authors get role-based access — a partner cannot see another partner's sales data or author earnings.
Protected now
